About Us

We are dedicated to revolutionizing skincare by leveraging the power of science. Our focus is on analyzing the skin microbiome and DNA to identify biomarkers for health and cosmetic applications. With personalized solutions tailored to each individual, we aim to enhance skin health and confidence.

Vision: To create a future where skincare is both scientifically advanced and deeply personal.

Mission: To combine cutting-edge research and technology to provide innovative skincare solutions.

Our Workflow

  1. Sample Collection: Non-invasive skin swabs are used to collect samples for analysis.
  2. DNA and Microbiome Sequencing: Using 16S rRNA and shotgun metagenomics to profile the skin microbiome and genetic material.
  3. Bioinformatics Analysis: Advanced tools process and analyze the sequencing data to derive actionable insights.
  4. Data Interpretation: Dermatologists and scientists identify microbial and genetic patterns to address skin concerns.
  5. Personalized Product Formulation: Customized skincare products are developed based on the findings.
  6. Product Testing: Products are tested for efficacy and safety, ensuring customer satisfaction.
  7. Customer Feedback: Continuous improvement through feedback and periodic data monitoring.
